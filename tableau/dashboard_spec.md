# Project 03 Tableau Dashboard Spec

## Dashboard Title

Healthcare Claims Processing Efficiency Dashboard

## Dashboard Pages

### 1. Executive Overview

Top KPI cards:

- Total claims
- Average processing days
- Approval rate
- Denial rate
- Rework rate
- Total paid amount

Charts:

- Monthly claims volume trend
- Average processing days by payer
- Denial rate by service line
- Turnaround bucket distribution

Business question: Are claims being processed efficiently, and where are delays concentrated?

### 2. Denial and Rework Drivers

Charts:

- Denied claims by denial reason
- Denied billed amount by payer
- Rework rate by service line
- Heatmap of payer by denial reason

Business question: Which operational issues are creating avoidable rework and revenue cycle friction?

### 3. Provider Delay Trends

Charts:

- Provider ranking by average processing days
- Provider denial rate vs. claim volume scatter plot
- Specialty-level turnaround comparison
- High operational risk provider table

Business question: Which providers or specialties need workflow review?

## Filters

- Submitted month
- Payer
- Provider specialty
- Service line
- Claim status
- State

## Color Strategy

- Navy for primary KPI text
- Teal for favorable values
- Amber for moderate risk
- Red for high denial or delay risk
- Light gray background for dashboard sections
